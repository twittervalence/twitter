Sentiment Analysis of Twitter Data

  • Vienna

  • About

    • This web-based application explores the sentiment of people in the cities of Vienna, Brussels and Munich from 2011-2018.The platform helps users understand what these cities look like and how different people react to different places and at different times of the year.

  • Goals
  • Encourage tourism
  • Help people understand the cities
  • Aid decision making
